Famous Math Quotes

  1. Pure mathematics is, in its way, the poetry of logical ideas.
  2. Mathematics is the music of reason.
  3. Mathematics is not about numbers, equations, computations, or algorithms: it is about understanding.
  4. Mathematics has beauty and romance. It’s not a boring place to be, the mathematical world. It’s an extraordinary place; it’s worth spending time there.
  5. To me, mathematics, computer science, and the arts are insanely related. They’re all creative expressions.
  6. Mathematics is a place where you can do things which you can’t do in the real world.
